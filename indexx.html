About Water Conservation

Water is a precious resource that is becoming scarcer each day. In many parts of the world, including Kenya, people face water shortages and have to find ways to conserve water in order to ensure that there is enough for everyone. Water conservation involves using water wisely and avoiding waste. There are many ways to conserve water, including fixing leaks, using water-efficient appliances, and adopting water-saving habits.

Our website provides information on water conservation and the steps you can take to save water. We also have infographics that show how water is used in Kenya and the effects of water shortage on the environment and people's lives. Additionally, we have a carousel that displays images of places that are experiencing water shortage, as well as a contact form that you can use to reach out to us if you have any questions or feedback.
